What Liste Does

Liste is a smart shopping app designed over 36 hours at Bitcamp, a hackathon at University of Maryland. Add items to your main weekly list to keep track of groceries to restock, and add items to your side one-off list for cool recipes you want to try out. When you're done, click 'Send Email' to get a weekly reminder!

Who We Are

Adam Almonte, Kyle Goodwill, Amanda Kobiolka, and Geoffrey Yip are four students who designed liste over a 36-hour period at Bitcamp, a hackathon at the University of Maryland. They represent HackBU, a learn-at-your-own-pace workshop where people are exposed to computer languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Go check them out!
